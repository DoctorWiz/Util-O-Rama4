Westside
Christmas on Crystal Springs
10580 Crystal Springs Road
Off of Chafee road near I-10
This is probably the biggest single light show in the Northeast Florida area. Winner of ABC's "Great Christmas Light Fight" in 2014! Over 90,000 lights on thousands of channels. M.C.ed by a giant robotic snowman. I have personally visited this display and it is awesome. There is a lot of traffic. For best viewing, park in the empty lot just west of the display and walk down the sidewalk and watch for a while!
